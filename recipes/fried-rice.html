Fried Rice

Fried rice is a dish of cooked rice that has been stir-fried in a wok or a frying pan and is usually mixed with other ingredients such as eggs, vegetables, seafood or meat. Fried rice is a popular component of East Asian and Southeast Asian cuisines.

Fried rice is a common street food in Asia. In some Asian contries, small restaurants, street vendors and travelling hawkers specialize in serving fried rice.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Eggs
  • Sesame oil
  • Soy sauce
  • Green onions
  • Peas
  • Carrots

Steps

  1. Cook rice using prefered method
  2. Oil up the pan and add veggies
  3. After 2 or 3 minutes add 2 whole eggs
  4. After 2 minutes add cooked rice in a pan
  5. Add soy sauce and sesame oil
  6. When fried add some green onions on top
