Protein Pancakes

Protein pancakes are one of the best ways to start your day. They are tasty and will increase your daily protein intake.

Not only are they healthy and tasty, but they are easy to prepare also!

Ingredients

  • 1 banana
  • 75g oats
  • 3 large eggs
  • 2 tbsp of milk
  • pinch of cinnamon
  • 1 scoop of protein powder
  • coconut oil
  • maple syrup and berries for serving

Steps

  1. Blend banana, oats, eggs, protein powder, baking powder in a blender until smooth
  2. Oil up the pan
  3. Pour the batter and cook for 1-2 minutes
  4. Serve with syrup and berries
